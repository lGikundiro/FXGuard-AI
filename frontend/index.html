Understanding risk levels

FXGuard AI compares recent exchange-rate changes with past BNR records, then groups the payment into Low, Medium, or High risk.

Low

The recent rate has stayed within its usual range.

  • Your quoted price is unlikely to need adjustment for this payment window.
  • Continue monitoring the rate normally as the payment date approaches.
  • No immediate action required — standard payment timing is fine.
Medium

The recent changes suggest this payment could become moderately more expensive.

  • Consider building a small buffer (5–10%) into your cost estimate.
  • Check the rate again closer to the payment date before confirming quotes.
  • If the invoice is large, a partial early payment may reduce exposure.
High

Recent changes suggest this payment could become noticeably more expensive.

  • Review payment timing — paying earlier (if cash flow allows) may reduce cost.
  • Increase your margin buffer or revise supplier quotes before committing.
  • Consider splitting the payment to reduce single-exposure risk.

These risk levels are for planning support only. They do not guarantee what the exchange rate will do next.
