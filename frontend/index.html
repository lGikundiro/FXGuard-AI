What the risk levels mean

We compare recent exchange rates with past BNR rates. The result is Low, Medium, or High risk.

Low

The recent rate has stayed within its usual range.

  • The estimate shows a smaller chance of added cost during the selected period.
  • Recent movements have been limited compared with the model's past data.
  • The rate can still change before payment.
Medium

Recent changes show that this payment may cost more.

  • The estimate shows a moderate chance of added cost during the selected period.
  • The displayed extra cost is a planning scenario, not a guaranteed future cost.
  • Payment timing, available cash and supplier terms remain business factors.
High

Recent changes show that this payment could cost much more.

  • The estimate shows a higher chance of added cost during the selected period.
  • The displayed extra cost shows the possible impact under the planning scenario.
  • The result does not decide when, how or whether to make the payment.

Use these risk levels for planning only. We cannot promise what the exchange rate will do next.
